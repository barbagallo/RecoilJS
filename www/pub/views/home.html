What is RecoilJS?

RecoilJS is a light-weight JavaScript framework that provides tools such as data-binding and routing, without getting in your way as a developer, or pushing any particular structure.

Why should I use RecoilJS?

The most important feature of RecoilJS is the architecture: Recoil was architected in such a way that it will not limit you as a developer. It provides a series of tools that you can use; however, it is totally up to you how much or how little you use.

Some other features:

Two-way data-binding
Data-binding is a way for you to link your DOM elements to your data. This eliminates the hassle of manually syncing your UI and data.
Templating
Recoil provides an easy templating syntax that gives you access to dynamic features such as logical blocks, iteration, and of course, text replacement. More importantly, these templates will intelligently re-render individual parts as your data changes.
Composition
Composition gives you a way to load in components as controller/view pairs. This is particularly useful for loading modules, or sections of your site. Of course, you can also load in standalone views, or choose a view and controller to load in together.
Routing
Recoil provides a simple router that lets you easily handle all hash changes to provide linkable URL's within single-page applications.
